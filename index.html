Note that whitespace has no effect in HTML. You can put your entire document on one line, though this is not recommended.

You can use tags to provide emphasis. You may also still see the old italic and bold tags still in use. You've probably also noticed that you're allow to nest tags inside one another.

This p tag has an "attribute" that makes it align to the right of the screen. Attributes are a useful way to change the behavior of a tag.
